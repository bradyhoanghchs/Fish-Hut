About Fish Hut

Fish Hut is a sea food restaurant located in the small town of Savannah, Tennessee, home to 7,019 people. We're locally owned and operated, and have many dishes to choose from. From fish to chicken, we've got plenty. Take a look at our menu, or come on by and see for yourself. Desserts change each day, so it's always a nice surprise.

Why Should You Visit?

Why should you visit? Why should you choose our restaurant instead of the Wendy's across the street? Simple; we're not a franchise. We're just a small little home town restaurant. We create our food from scratch, meaning we're not going to just go and heat up some chicken and serve it to you. Our prices are also great; (Around the $$ range) for what you get. So come on by! We're open on Thursdays, Fridays, and Saturdays from 10:30 A.M - 8:30 P.M.
